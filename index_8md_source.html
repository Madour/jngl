1 # Just a Neat Game Library {#mainpage}
2 
3 JNGL is an easy to use C++/Python game library for Linux, Windows, macOS, Android, iOS and the
4 Nintendo Switch<sup><a href="#fn1">1</a></sup>.
5 
6 * [GitHub](https://github.com/jhasse/jngl)
7 
8 ## Features
9 
10 * Modern C++14 style
11 * [zlib License](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Zlib_license), so suitable for commercial use and
12  static linking
13 * OpenGL accelerated
14 * PNG, JPEG, BMP and [WebP](https://developers.google.com/speed/webp/) image support
15 * Ogg Vorbis audio support
16 * UTF-8 text output using [FreeType](http://www.freetype.org/)
17
